Nearing

Beautiful countdown timers for the moments that matter.

About

Nearing helps you count down to your important events with a clean, beautiful interface. Share countdowns with friends, customize colors, and add widgets to your home screen.

Features

  • Create countdowns for any date
  • Recurring events (weekly, monthly, yearly)
  • Custom colors and emoji
  • Share countdowns with friends
  • Home screen widgets
  • iCloud sync across devices

Support

Having an issue or have a suggestion? Send a message and we'll get back to you.

Nearing does not collect any personal data. Your countdowns are stored on your device and synced via your iCloud account.
