Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

Nearing is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Storage

Your countdowns are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you are signed into iCloud, your data is synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit. This sync is handled entirely by Apple and we have no access to your iCloud data.

Data Collection

Nearing does not collect any personal data, usage analytics, or tracking information. We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising frameworks.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Notifications

Nearing may send local notifications to remind you of upcoming countdowns. These notifications are scheduled on your device and no data is sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

Nearing does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs beyond Apple's own frameworks (CloudKit, StoreKit, WidgetKit).

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the support page.
