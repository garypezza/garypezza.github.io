License Quest

Spot every plate on the road.

About

License Quest is the classic road trip license plate game for your iPhone and iPad. Track plates from all 50 US states, Washington DC, and 13 Canadian provinces and territories on an interactive map. Tap a state to mark it found, watch it animate, and work toward unlocking achievements along the way.

Features

🗺️

Interactive Map

Tap states and provinces directly on the map with pinch-to-zoom.

🏆

Achievements

Unlock milestones, regional awards, and the ultimate completion badge.

🇺🇸

US & Canada

🎉
